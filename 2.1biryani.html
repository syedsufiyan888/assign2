Recipe for preapring Biryani

  1. 2 cups Basmati rice (soaked)
  2. 500g chicken or lamb (cut into pieces)
  3. 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  4. 3 tomatoes (chopped)
  5. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  6. 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  7. 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  8. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  10. 1 tablespoon biryani masala
  11. 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  12. 1 cinnamon stick
  13. 3-4 cardamom pods
  14. 4-5 cloves
  15. 1 bay leaf
  16. Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  17. Juice of 1 lemon
  18. Salt to taste
  19. Water (for cooking rice)
