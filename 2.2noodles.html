Recipe for preapring Noodles

  1. 200g noodles (any type, boiled)
  2. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  3. 1 onion (sliced)
  4. 1 carrot (julienned)
  5. 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  6. 1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)
  7. 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  8. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  9. 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)
  10. 1 tablespoon vinegar
  11. 1 teaspoon black pepper
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Spring onions (chopped, for garnish)
