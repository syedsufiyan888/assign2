Recipe for preapring Fried rice

  1. 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  2. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  3. 1 onion (chopped)
  4. 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  5. 1 carrot (diced)
  6. 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  7. 1/4 cup bell peppers (chopped)
  8. 2 eggs (scrambled separately)
  9. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  10. 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  11. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Spring onions (chopped, for garnish)
